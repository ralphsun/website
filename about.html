About Me

Sir Ralph Sun, Lord of Soylent

Hello! My name is Ralph Sun, and I am a rising sophomore and Presidental Scholar at the University of Southern California. I am majoring in computer science with a minor in mathematics. Prior to USC, I attended the Mathematics & Science Academy at Ocean Lakes High School in Virginia Beach, VA.

I am a full-stack developer and tech enthusiast. Most of my recent work has focused around web development and scripting, but I am always trying to learn new skills. I love learning about up-and-coming teachnologies, and I am particularly interested in artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, and cryptocurrency. As a big fan of HBO's Silicon Valley, I am fascinated with startup culture. I am also an frequent attendee of hackathons.

I also have many interests outside of computer science. I enjoy all sports, but I follow golf and college football the closest. I am passionate about learning and self-improvement, and I try my hardest to be a better person each day. Recently, I have been learning more about cars, investing, medieval history, and psychology. In addition to these interests, I also enjoy exploring the outdoors, traveling, and working-out.
